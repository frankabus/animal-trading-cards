Shiba Inu

Shiba inu rarely bark. Instead, a special, and often surprising, unique characteristic the breed is the "shiba scream", a high-pitched yelp or howl used in times of distress.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 18-22 lb
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Common Colors: Red, sesame, black and tan, or white

The shiba inu is a small-to-medium-sized dog originating from Japan. Independent by nature, these dogs are notoriously stubborn and are generally wary of strangers. Once trained and bonded, however, the shiba is very loyal. Shiba inu are also known for being meticulous self-groomers.
